First, identify each of the flowers. They all happen to be the state flower or state wildflower of a US state (several flowers are the state flowers of multiple states, but we’re confident solvers will be able to resolve any ambiguities). Note that each of the state abbreviations corresponds to a chemical element (this is subtly clued by the title: Sherlock Holmes is famous for saying “Elementary! My dear Watson”). Take the atomic number of the element, say i, and find the ith appointed US Supreme Court justice (this is also clued by the title, as Oliver Wendell Holmes was a famous Supreme Court justice). Index the year the state joined the union into the justice’s last name (wrapping around if necessary, and zero-indexing) to produce the answer: WAIANAE TUB. The title contains the state flower of Hawaii, which makes sense since Waianae is in Hawaii.
